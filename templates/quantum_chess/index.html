How to Play

Rules
  • The board is a regular 8x8 chessboard with 16 pieces each side
  • Press Shift to toggle quantum mode
  • In quantum mode, you can split a piece into two positions
  • When a piece moves through a blocked square, pieces become entangled
  • Measuring (capturing or being captured) collapses the quantum state
Quantum Moves
  • Split: Move a piece to two different squares simultaneously
  • Entangle: Two pieces become quantumly linked
  • Measure: Capturing a piece reveals its actual position
